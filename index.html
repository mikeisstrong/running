Three steps, then it's fully automatic every time you open it.

Step 01
Strava credentials
Create a free Strava API app at strava.com/settings/api — use this page's URL as the redirect. Then paste your Client ID and Client Secret below.
Step 02
Anthropic API key
Paste your key from console.anthropic.com — it stays in your browser only and powers the daily briefing.
Step 03
When is your race?
Pick your target race date. The 12-week plan builds backward from it — the goal is fixed, the timeline is flexible.
You're set up.
